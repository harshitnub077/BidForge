REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
Project Name: Cloud Infrastructure Modernization
Company: GlobalTech Innovations
Date: July 15, 2026

1. Introduction
GlobalTech Innovations is seeking a strategic partner to modernize our legacy on-premise infrastructure. We are transitioning to a cloud-native architecture to improve scalability, reduce operational costs, and enhance our disaster recovery capabilities.

2. Scope of Work
- Migration of 50+ legacy applications to the cloud (AWS or GCP).
- Setup of a secure, compliant cloud networking environment (SOC 2 and GDPR compliant).
- Implementation of automated CI/CD pipelines.
- 24/7 monitoring and managed support for the first 12 months.

3. Key Pain Points
- Our current infrastructure experiences high latency during peak global traffic hours.
- We lack automated scaling, which leads to unnecessary downtime.
- Existing disaster recovery processes are manual and slow.

4. Requirements
- The vendor must have a proven track record in enterprise cloud migrations.
- The proposed solution must guarantee 99.99% uptime.
- Migration must be completed within a 6-month timeframe with zero data loss.

5. Proposal Guidelines
Please submit a proposal outlining your technical approach, implementation timeline, security framework, and a detailed pricing breakdown.
